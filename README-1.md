# PayFlow GRC Programme — Fintech Security Governance Simulation

This repository documents an 8-week, end-to-end Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC) engagement for **PayFlow**, a simulated Lagos-based fintech startup processing card, bank transfer, and mobile wallet payments. The scenario models a fast-growing payments company with no prior security governance — informal access controls, unmanaged secrets, and zero documented policy — and walks through the full lifecycle of standing up an Information Security Management System (ISMS) from the ground up.

## Frameworks Referenced

- ISO/IEC 27001:2022
- PCI DSS v4.0
- Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023
- NDPC General Application and Implementation Directive (GAID) 2025
- CBN Risk-Based Cybersecurity Framework 2024
- Nigeria Cybercrimes Act (Amended 2024)

## Deliverables

| # | Document | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Risk Register** | Asset inventory, threat identification, and initial qualitative risk register |
| 2 | **Risk Assessment** | Formal 5×5 Likelihood × Impact scoring and heat-map analysis |
| 3 | **Security Policy Suite** | Password Policy and Access Control Policy (RBAC matrix, provisioning/deprovisioning, PAM controls) |
| 4 | **ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Compliance Checklist** | Full gap assessment across ISMS clauses (4–10) and all Annex A controls |
| 5 | **Internal Audit Report** | Simulated ISO 27001 internal audit producing classified nonconformities (Major/Minor) and Opportunities for Improvement |
| 6 | **Risk Treatment Plan** | Treatment strategies, control mapping, residual risk projections, and an 8-week remediation roadmap |
| 7 | **Executive Compliance Report** | Board-level synthesis of risk posture, audit findings, and regulatory exposure |
| 8 | **Security Governance Plan** | Consolidating ISMS foundation document: scope statement, policy statement, governance RACI, KPIs, operational calendar, and certification roadmap |

## Programme Arc

The programme traces PayFlow's posture from an undocumented, reactive security environment through structured risk identification, policy development, audit simulation, and remediation planning — modeling the kind of work a GRC analyst would produce ahead of a real ISO 27001 Stage 1 certification audit.

All deliverables reference a consistent risk register (R-001–R-012) and control mapping across documents, so findings, audit nonconformities, treatment actions, and executive reporting all trace back to the same source data.

## Disclaimer

PayFlow is a fictional company created for scenario-based learning. All findings, risk scores, and audit results in this repository are simulated and do not reference any real organisation.

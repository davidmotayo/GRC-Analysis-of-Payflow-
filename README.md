# PayFlow GRC Programme — Fintech Security Governance Simulation

This repository documents an 8-week, end-to-end Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC) engagement for PayFlow, a simulated Lagos-based fintech startup processing card, bank transfer, and mobile wallet payments. The scenario models a fast-growing payments company with no prior security governance — informal access controls, unmanaged secrets, and zero documented policy — and walks through the full lifecycle of standing up an Information Security Management System (ISMS) from the ground up.

Frameworks referenced: ISO/IEC 27001:2022, PCI DSS v4.0, and Nigerian regulatory obligations under the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023, the NDPC's GAID 2025, the CBN Risk-Based Cybersecurity Framework 2024, and the Nigeria Cybercrimes Act (Amended 2024).

Deliverables produced:
Asset & Risk Register — asset inventory, threat identification, and an initial qualitative risk register
Risk Assessment — formal 5×5 Likelihood × Impact scoring and heat-map analysis
Security Policy Suite — Password Policy and Access Control Policy (RBAC matrix, provisioning/deprovisioning, PAM controls)
ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Compliance Checklist — full gap assessment across ISMS clauses (4–10) and all Annex A controls
Internal Audit Findings Report — a simulated ISO 27001 internal audit producing classified nonconformities (Major/Minor) and Opportunities for Improvement
Risk Treatment Plan — treatment strategies, control mapping, residual risk projections, and an 8-week remediation roadmap
Executive Compliance Report — board-level synthesis of risk posture, audit findings, and regulatory exposure
Security Governance Plan — the consolidating ISMS foundation document: scope statement, policy statement, governance RACI, KPIs, operational calendar, and certification roadmap
The programme traces PayFlow's posture from an undocumented, reactive security environment through structured risk identification, policy development, audit simulation, and remediation planning — modeling the kind of work a GRC analyst would produce ahead of a real ISO 27001 Stage 1 certification audit.
